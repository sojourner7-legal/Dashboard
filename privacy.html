Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Sojourner7 Dashboard application ("the Application") handles data. The Application is a personal-use tool operated exclusively by Richard Brown ("Operator") for managing his own Sojourner7 social media accounts and connected commerce store. There is no other user of the Application.

1. Information We Collect

The Application stores the following information, all of which belongs to the Operator:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens issued by YouTube (Google), Instagram (Meta), TikTok, and Shopify, tied to the Operator's own accounts on those platforms.
  • Post drafts, scheduled posts, media metadata, and audit logs entered by the Operator.
  • Product catalog data fetched from the Operator's own Shopify store, used to enrich auto-generated post drafts.
  • Aggregate analytics returned by the connected platforms (view counts, engagement metrics) for the Operator's own posts.

2. How We Use the Information

All collected information is used solely to operate the Application's posting and analytics workflows for the Operator's own accounts. No information is sold, shared with advertisers, or processed for any third-party purpose.

3. Where the Information Is Stored

All data is stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database on hardware controlled by the Operator (a personal Mac Mini server). OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM with a key held in environment variables. The Application's network exposure is restricted to the Operator's private Tailscale network.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Data is transmitted only to the platforms to which it pertains: posts and media are sent to YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, or Shopify as part of fulfilling the Operator's publishing requests. The Application uses no analytics services, advertising networks, error-reporting services, or other third-party data processors.

5. Data Retention

Audit logs are retained for 30 days and then automatically purged. OAuth tokens are stored for the lifetime of the connection and are deleted when the Operator disconnects a platform. Post drafts are retained until manually deleted.

6. Your Rights

As the Application is operated by and for the Operator alone, the Operator retains full control of all data and can delete it at any time by removing the local database or disconnecting platforms via the Application's settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to or used by children under 13. No data from children is collected.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Policy may be updated from time to time. The current version is always available at this URL.
